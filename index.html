Frequently Asked Questions

Still deciding? Here's what most people ask before registering.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend? +
Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to a colleague at no extra cost.
Is there a virtual or streaming option? +
DevConf 2026 is an in-person-only event this year. All Pro and Team tickets include lifetime access to recordings after the conference.
Do you offer student or group discounts? +
Yes — students get 50% off Standard tickets with a valid .edu email, and groups of 5 or more qualify for a 15% discount on Team tickets.
What's the difference between Standard and Pro? +
Pro adds unlimited workshop access, the speaker meet & greet, the VIP networking dinner, lifetime recordings, and a 1-on-1 mentorship session.
Is there a code of conduct? +
Yes, all attendees, speakers, and sponsors are expected to follow our code of conduct, available on the event policies page.
